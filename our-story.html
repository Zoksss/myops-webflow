Why MyOps
Our story
MyOps was born from a deep understanding of the challenges that real estate professionals face every day. Created by a seasoned COO and a leading luxury real estate agent, we saw firsthand how agents and teams struggled with scattered systems, endless spreadsheets, and time-consuming administrative work that pulled them away from what truly matters, building relationships and closing deals.
​
We knew there had to be a better way. By combining operational expertise with frontline industry experience, we created a solution that bridges the gap between efficiency and productivity. MyOps was designed to take the complexity out of real estate operations, offering an all-in-one platform that automates workflows, provides real-time financial insights, and ensures that every critical piece of information, whether it's property details, contracts, passwords, or team notes, is always at your fingertips.
​
From solo agents to large teams, our goal is to redefine how real estate businesses operate, making them smarter, faster, and more scalable. What started as a vision to streamline operations has now become an industry-leading AI-powered platform that helps professionals focus on what they do best—growing their business and delivering an exceptional client experience.
​
Welcome to the future of real estate operations. Welcome to MyOps.
